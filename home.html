About Us


Founded in 2010, Sakura Sushi & Sake serves a fusion of modern and traditional Japanese Cuisine. We prioritise excellent quality of food and service, while creating a comfortable yet elevated dining experience.

The Founder


Sakura Sushi & Sake is founded by Chef Akira Haru in 2006. Born in Kanazawa, Chef Haru has always been fascinated by the art and history of sushi making. This has lead to his dream of owning a Japanese restuarant.

Drawing inspiration from both the modern and traditional Japanese culture, Sakura Sushi & Sake is a stage for him to showcase his passion and authentic creation.
